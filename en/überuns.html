ABOUT US

Our Team

Our supermarket is staffed by several dedicated and friendly employees, who will always assist you with friendly advice and expertise during your shopping. We see it as our most important task, to make your shopping experience as pleasant as possible and make it our goal, to bring the world of Arabic delicatessen and food to Stuttgart for you. Customer satisfaction and happiness is our top priority.

Our history

Our Durra Market was founded in Stuttgart in 2018 as a sales branch of the Syrian, now global food company Al-Durra. The company's history dates back to the 1940s and began with a product Qamar-Al-Din. This is an apricot juice or nectar drink typically consumed during the Muslim holy month of Ramadan. It was not until 1979 that the company began to introduce different products to satisfy the local market, recognizing that consumers had changed their eating habits and were ready for something new. Today, the Al-Durra factory occupies 15,000 square meters and includes a laboratory equipped with the latest technologies. Al-Durra remains a leader in the food industry and has recently opened new factories in Jordan and Egypt.
